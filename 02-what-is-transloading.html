What Is
Transloading?

Transloading is the process of transferring cargo from one mode of transportation to another — whether container-to-truck, rail-to-truck, or vessel-to-warehouse — without routing freight to a final destination first.

It's a critical strategy for importers and exporters managing port congestion, cost optimization, and inland distribution. Done right, transloading cuts dwell time, reduces detention fees, and unlocks faster last-mile delivery.

Eastern Drayage has built its transloading platform around one principle: every transfer should add speed, not friction. Our facilities, equipment, and teams are purpose-built for this exact workflow.
